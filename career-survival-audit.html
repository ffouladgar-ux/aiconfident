The Prompt

ROLE: You are Codie Sanchez, entrepreneur, investor, and author of Main Street Millionaire. CONTEXT: I want to audit my role through the lens of AI and automation. Assume that someone could buy an incredibly capable AI assistant for $200 per month. My goal is not to replace myself—it's to identify where I create uniquely human value and where I'm spending time on work that is becoming increasingly substitutable. REQUEST: First, interview me to fully understand my job. Ask about: - My role and responsibilities - How I spend a typical week - Decisions only I can make - Work that repeats frequently - Meetings, communication, analysis, creative work, leadership, sales, operations, hiring, customer relationships, strategy, and execution After gathering enough information, create a "Career Survival Audit" with these sections: 1. Work AI could likely perform today with minimal supervision. 2. Work AI could perform if I designed better systems. 3. Work that still requires uniquely human judgment, taste, relationships, negotiation, creativity, leadership, or trust. 4. The top 20% of activities that make me disproportionately valuable. 5. The bottom 20% of activities I should automate, delegate, eliminate, or redesign first. 6. Blind spots where I may be overestimating my uniqueness. 7. Skills that will become dramatically more valuable over the next five years. 8. A 90-day plan to shift more of my calendar toward irreplaceable work. Finish by giving me a "Replaceability Score" from 1–100, explain exactly how you calculated it, and recommend three specific changes that would improve that score the fastest. QUESTION: Please ask any clarifying questions.

How to Interpret Your Score

1–30

Highly Substitutable. Your role has significant overlap with what AI can already do. The 90-day plan is critical — follow it.

31–60

Mixed Risk. Some parts of your role are irreplaceable, but you're spending time on work AI could absorb. The bottom 20% list is your priority.

61–85

Strong Position. You have real human value. Focus on amplifying the top 20% and building systems around the rest.

86–100

AI-Resistant. Your role is built on judgment, trust, and relationships that AI cannot replicate. Use your position to lead AI adoption in your field.

Watch Out For

  • The AI will be polite. Push it for specifics — ask for examples
  • The replaceability score is directional, not absolute. Run it quarterly
  • Share it with your team. The most dangerous person is the one who hasn't run this audit yet
